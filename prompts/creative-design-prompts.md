## üí° Creative Prompt Engineering Tips

### 1. **Be Specific About Style**
- "Minimalist 8-bit pixel style" vs "Pixel art"
- "Professional studio lighting" vs "Good lighting"
- "Vector art with bold colors" vs "Colorful drawing"

### 2. **Include Technical Details**
- Medium: "Digital painting", "3D render", "Vector illustration"
- Lighting: "Soft natural lighting", "Dramatic studio lighting"
- Composition: "Centered composition", "Rule of thirds"
- Color: "Pastel color palette", "High contrast colors"

### 3. **Add Context and Story**
- Who is this for?
- What is the purpose?
- What emotion should it convey?
- Where will it be used?

### 4. **Iterate and Refine**
- Start with a basic concept
- Add details progressively
- Test variations with different elements
- Keep what works, discard what doesn't
